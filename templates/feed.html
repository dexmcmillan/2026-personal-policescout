About this tool

Police Scout automatically monitors the websites of {{ sources|length }} Canadian police services and collects press releases as they're published. It also ingests real-time call data from the Toronto Police Service (TPS) Major Incident Portal. The feed is rebuilt roughly every hour via a scheduled GitHub Actions workflow.

Press release body text is scraped from each agency's website and stored locally. TPS calls are pulled directly from the TPS open data feed and include the incident type, police division, and nearest cross-streets.

White card — press release from a police service website
Blue card — live TPS major incident call
